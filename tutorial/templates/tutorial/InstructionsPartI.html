Part I consists of X rounds that each have 3 stages.

In the first stage, the auction, each employed can make one wage offer and each worker can accept one of these offers. Once accepted, the respective employer and worker are matched for the remaining stages of a round.

In the second stage, extra taxes and wage adjustments are possible.

In the third stage, workers participate in a real effort task.

Each participant is given a role of a worker or an employer after the instructions. Participant will keep the same role for the entire duration of the experiment.
